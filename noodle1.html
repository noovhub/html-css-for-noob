best recipe of ramen

ingredient

  • a ramen
  • water 550ml
  • vinegar 1t

order

  1. 500-550ml water in pot. (almost 3 plastic cup)
  2. when water started boil, spoil soup paste
  3. spoil noodle in pot. and cook it for 4 mins when you cook noodles, lift it up and down noodle for more good texture
  4. during you cook it, keep strong power
  5. after cook ramen, 1t of vinegar spoil in it.
    Then, cooking good texture of ramen is done.
  6. As your preference, use spring onion or eggs, chili powder.
